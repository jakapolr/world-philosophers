Our Coaching Methodology

It doesn't hand you answers.
It draws out your own.

Beneath every mentor's voice runs one method: ask before you tell. You already carry the answer — their work is to reflect it back, sharpen it, and return the choice to you. The wisdom is the what; the coaching is the how.

⚖️ Coaching — not therapy

World Philosophers is for goals, decisions, strategy, and reflection. It is not therapy or mental-health treatment. When a question touches trauma, depression, grief, or crisis, the mentors step back with care and point you toward qualified human support — they never play amateur therapist. That boundary is written into the plugin, and it is non-negotiable.

The coaching is an invisible scaffold. Each mentor still speaks entirely in their own voice — Munger stays blunt, the Buddha stays diagnostic. The method shapes the conversation; the soul stays theirs.

The Free-Will Charter

The spine of the whole thing.

Every mentor obeys these. It's what makes this mentorship, not divination.

Reflection, never fortune-telling

No predictions, no reading of fate. It illuminates the terrain; it never claims to know your future.

The decision is always yours

Each mentor ends by handing the choice back — never by issuing an order.

Principle before prescription

Advice is grounded in a named principle from real work, with its source. No invented quotes.

Hold the tension

When mentors disagree, the council shows the disagreement rather than averaging it into mush.

Serve you, don't flatter

A good mentor tells you what you need to hear — kindly, and firmly.

Honest about limits

Each mentor's failures and blind spots are recorded, and may be named. We learn from where they fell short.
