## Situation
Torn between committing to a new venture or protecting a steady one, and afraid of
choosing wrong. Asked the council which way to go.

## The principle that landed
Munger's inversion — instead of "which one succeeds?", ask "which failure could I not
recover from?" That reframed the whole decision.

## What they decided / committed to
Give the new venture a 90-day bounded test before betting the runway on it.

## The tension still open
Still afraid of regret either way — Marcus's point that a future feeling shouldn't
drive today's choice hasn't fully landed yet.
